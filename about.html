About the Creators

Val & Caleb are 2D Digital Illustrators, aspiring Storyboard Animators, and Character Designers. Val has been drawing original characters and fanart since he was 13. Caleb specializes in drawing furry characters and is clearly gay. They both attended Ivy Tech Communtity College earning an Associates Degree in Fine Arts and Visual Communications, respectively. Currently, they are both studying Media Arts in Digital Storytelling at Indiana University Indianapolis.
